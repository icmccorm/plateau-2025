PLATEAU will take place on the Carnegie Mellon University campus in Pittsburgh, PA during Spring 2023.

Attending PLATEAU is free and open to the public. However, you must register in advance. An online registration form will be available soon!

We've reserved a block of rooms at the Hilton Garden Inn University Place. The room rate is $135/night, and to take advantage of the block, attendees must book by January 13th, 2023. A link to reserve rooms will be available shortly.
