PLATEAU will take place on the Carnegie Mellon University campus in Pittsburgh, PA on November 9, 2021. The workshop will be held in the Collaborative Innovation Center in the Panther Hollow Room (4th floor, Room 4105).

This is a draft schedule for planning purposes! Expect small tweaks as we approach the week of the workshop!
